FOOD AND BAVERAGES

Nasi Kandar Deen Maju

One of the best nasi kandar in town. The price is affordable. But, you have to be patience as you need to queue and wait for your turn. Must try if you visit Penang. Worth the long queue.

Restoran Kapitan (Chulia Street)

All the food there was delicious especially the popular briyani claypot rice, chicken milani and cheese nan. This is the principle menu that is always ordered by visitors.

Teochew Chendol, Penang Road

A small bowl of cendol with average price (RM3/bowl). It is a must to try out when you are in Georgetown. It is definetly a great bowl of cendol.

Mee Sotong Hameed Pata

Must try the mee sotong in Padang Kota Lama. Simply Delicious. Finish off with a glass of coconut shake, the combination is so amazing. Both were so good and addicting.

Padang Brown Food Court

Padang Brown Food Court is one of best place to eat in Penang. There are variaties of delicious and legend food in Penang that we can enjoy with our family and friends.

D'seafood Paradise

This is not the high-end fine dining experience, but fantastic tasting seafood at a very reasonable price. If you are looking for a variety of delicious seafood and don't care about the very basic setting of the restaurant and not so nice restrooms, then this is the place to go.

Ais Tingkap

Had to try it as its only served here. The coconut water, rose syrup and basil seeds do make a nice combo. Thirst quencher on a hot sunny day. Try it once but be prepared for a long queue as they do the drinks in batches.

Sani Char Koey Teow

A very Good Malay style Char Koay Teow. The taste is fantastic. Try the Special with an extra egg on top. Open air hawker style. Really tasty. Totally different from the Chinese style. Highly recommend for anyone staying or passing by here.
